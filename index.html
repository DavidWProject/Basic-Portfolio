About Me

Evan Simon Ross is as ESL instructor working at Be Fluent NYC in Manhattan. He specializes in pronunciation and teaching comparative grammar to adult students whose first language is Japanese. He is a member of the Dartmouth College class of 2013 and graduated Summa Cum Laude with a major in Asian and Middle Eastern Languages and Literatures and minors in Music and Linguistics.

This portfolio page highlights some of Evan's diverse projects, including those related to ESL instruction as well as some of Evan's other professional and avocational interests: computer programming, music and writing. Evan is currently studying full-stack web development at the first-ever Columbia University Coding Bootcamp. This portfolio page is the fist of many web development projects completed as part of this program.
